PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana (PM-KISAN)

This scheme provides ₹6,000 annually in three equal installments to small and marginal farmers, aiming to supplement their income and support agricultural activities.

Atal Bhujal Yojana (Atal Jal)

Launched in 2019, this scheme focuses on sustainable groundwater management in seven states, including Rajasthan. It promotes community participation to improve water use efficiency and recharge groundwater levels

Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)

PM-JAY is a national public health insurance scheme that offers free access to secondary and tertiary healthcare services for low-income families. It aims to cover 10 crore families, providing cashless and paperless treatment at empaneled hospitals.

Scheme for First-Time Entrepreneurs

This initiative offers term loans up to ₹2 crore for five years to 5 lakh women, SC, and ST entrepreneurs. It also includes skill development programs to support new business ventures.

SWAMIH Fund 2

A ₹15,000 crore fund aimed at completing 1 lakh affordable and mid-income housing units. The fund focuses on finishing 50,000 stressed housing units by 2025, benefiting homebuyers facing delays in possession.
